One day at lunch, today, Andrea Lindner changed the course of human history. Upon discovering she had only spent $8.7 of Synchrony's money she did what any rational person would do: buy a useless bottle of jam to reach $10. 50 cents later she now possessed what no one would ever need. This website is dedicated her memory and the sacrifices she made that will likely result in the dicontinuement of Synchrony's $10 lunch vouchers program for Girls Who Code.
